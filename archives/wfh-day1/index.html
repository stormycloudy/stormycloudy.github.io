First official WFH day

2020-03-11 in blogs

To (efficiently) work in the time of coronavirus is somewhat a tricky thing. It's interesting because I found myself more effective and more productive while working from home. I get distracted a lot less, and therefore am able to focus on work better.

Or maybe it's only because I just started. I have to admit that I was kinda excited to get prepared for stuff -- stocking food and supplies for the following month. We went to grocery shopping (for the last time!!) this morning. I didn't really count our stocks and plan for daily consumption, but hopefully what we have now can last till mid-April. I don't know how my friends trapped in China felt when they got to know that they'd be "grounded" for an unlimited period of time, but well, there's some excitement in my preparations. ;p

BTW the cat didn't come to bother me even once. I have mixed feelings.
